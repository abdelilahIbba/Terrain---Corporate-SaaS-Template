About Terrain

We're on a mission to simplify business operations for companies of all sizes.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Terrain began with a simple observation: businesses were spending too much time on operational tasks and not enough on their core mission.

Our team of experienced developers and business operations experts came together to create a platform that automates the tedious work, so our customers can focus on what matters most.

Today, we serve thousands of businesses across various industries, helping them streamline operations, reduce costs, and drive growth.
